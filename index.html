Initial situation

I knew nothing about German language before starting to study it, except for a few common words as Hallo, Bier, auf wiedersehen, kaputt and a few others.

Learning tools

To learn German I'm using the following tools:

  • Grammar books: to get a grip on language grammar and common sentences;
  • Apps: like Duolingo or HelloTalk;
  • Books: to see how German is actually used by those who speak it;
  • Spotify: starting to listen to a lot of songs in German;
  • Youtube: watching videos in German, using subtitles to grasp the words;
  • Other: like changing my phone and PC language settings to German, or forcing myself to "think" in German.

I plan to write in depth commentaries for each of these tools, based on my personal experience and my thoughts on the matter.
